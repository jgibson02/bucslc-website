What We Do

We are a community of involved Computer Science students.

Robotics

Every week on Wednesdays from 4:15 - 5:15 in BF Hardware Lab, we work together to build and program Raspberry Pi powered robots. Using Python, Java, or a myriad of other choices, we will be looking at various uses for controlling the robots for future competitions.

LAN Parties

Hosted at least once a semester, we get together to socialize through video games. Games of any platform and era are welcome, however if it's not couch co-op we ask that it be shareware or F2P.

Tutoring

Coordinating with ACM, we help to provide tutoring for CS students every week on Thursdays from 3 to 7. We can also do one-on-one tutoring by appointment, and organizing study sessions before major homeworks and exams.

Networking

Getting involved with the CS LC means surrounding yourself with other motivated people in the department. This includes your own peers, knowledgeable upperclassmen, alumni, and faculty, leading to bigger opportunities down the road.
